About sornuAI

sornuAI is an AI powered platform with that predicts the accurate Nitrogen,Potassium and Phosphorus concentrations in soil, and by prediction,through the service of registered Agronomists working remotely via this platform, recommend appropriate fertilizer needs to meet specific needs per acre of land as per the needs of a specific crop. Together with NUTRISENSE; an IOT device that measures the soil pH, Soil electrical conductivity (EC) and soil moisture. sornuAI uses these parameters to predict nutrient levels

sornuAI works with existing soil moisture and nutrient detection devices.
